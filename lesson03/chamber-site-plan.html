Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

for the city of Spingville, Utah

Site Name: www.springvilleareachamberofcommerce.com

Overview

Purpose

The Springville Chamber of Commerce hopes to build the economy in our town, and the intent of this webpage is promote that. We want to attract businesses to join, provide opportunities for marketing and outreach, create awareness for events, and highlight businesses that show promise and growth.

Scenarios

  • What community events are taking place this month that I might be able to market my business at?
  • Who is the director of events and how can I get in contact with them?
  • What are the different benefits I can expect for becoming a member?
